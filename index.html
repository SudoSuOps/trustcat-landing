Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know this is legitimate?

Every second opinion is reviewed and digitally signed by a state-licensed dentist. We use blockchain technology to cryptographically verify that your X-rays haven't been altered and that a real licensed professional reviewed your case. You can verify the entire chain of custody on-chain.

What if I don't have digital X-rays?

You can take a photo of physical X-rays with your phone, or ask your dentist for digital copies. Most modern dental offices provide digital X-rays. We accept all standard formats.

How long does it take?

Most reviews are completed within 24 hours. You'll receive an email notification when your report is ready. Complex cases may take up to 48 hours.

Is my information private?

Yes. We're fully HIPAA compliant. Your X-rays are stored on decentralized infrastructure (IPFS) with encryption. Only you, the reviewing dentist, and our AI system can access your images. No personal identifying information is stored on the blockchain.

What happens after I get the second opinion?

You'll receive a detailed report explaining our dentist's findings. You can share this with your primary dentist or use it to make an informed decision about your treatment. We don't provide treatment - just an independent professional opinion.
