Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between free and verified?

The free tier gives you AI-powered analysis of your X-rays with educational findings - perfect for getting a second data point. The verified tier ($49) adds a state-licensed dentist review, professional clinical opinion, digital signature, and blockchain verification - giving you a legal second opinion you can use with confidence.

Can I upgrade from free to verified later?

Absolutely! Start with the free AI analysis. If you want the dentist verification, you can upgrade anytime for $49. Your AI analysis is already done, so the dentist just needs to review and sign off.

Is the free tier really free?

Yes! The AI analysis is completely free. We appreciate voluntary donations to support our mission, but they're never required. We built the free tier to make dental second opinions accessible to everyone.

Which states have licensed dentists?

We're building our network of licensed dentists across all 50 states. When you request verification, we'll match you with a dentist licensed in your state. If your state isn't covered yet, we'll notify you when coverage becomes available.

How do I know this is legitimate?

For verified reports, every opinion is reviewed and digitally signed by a state-licensed dentist (we provide their license number and state). We use blockchain technology to cryptographically verify that your X-rays haven't been altered and that a real licensed professional reviewed your case. You can verify the entire chain of custody on-chain.

Is my information private?

Yes. We're fully HIPAA compliant. Your X-rays are encrypted and stored on decentralized infrastructure (IPFS). Only you, the reviewing dentist (verified tier only), and our AI system can access your images. No personal identifying information is stored on the blockchain - only cryptographic hashes for verification.
